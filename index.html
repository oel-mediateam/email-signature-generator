Instructions

  1. Click the Get Signature! to copy the email signature to the computer's clipboard.
  2. Paste, Cmd(⌘)-V or Ctrl-V, the email signature to your email client.

For instructions on how to add signature to your email client, click the applicable links below.

  • Microsoft Outlook
  • Microsoft Outlook 365 (the web app)
  • Apple Mail

    The process to add a HTML signature to Apple Mail by copy and paste is complicated as it may involves system configurations. However, it can be done manually by following this how-to guide by lifewire.

Formatting and image will not be retained if the email client is composing messages in plain or rich text format. By default, Microsoft Outlook is composing in HTML format.
